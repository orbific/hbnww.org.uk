Hebden Bridge Networked Writing Workshop

The Hebden Bridge Networked Writing Workshop (HBNWW) is a creative writing workshop that explores the impact of the Internet on creative writing.

ChatGPT and Creative Writing

Remote session: June 26th 2023, 19:00h BST Free

This session will take place remotely. We will look at some of the ways in which AI can work with creative writing (both poetry and prose). We will be looking at both the risks and opportunities of these new tools, and working on a number of practical exercises with which to explore them.

The workshop will be led by me, James Burt, a writer and programmer. While I make no claims to being an AI expert, I’ve been playing with these tools and hope the group will provide an opportunity to come together as a group to discuss their potential.

A sign-up list for the session is available on eventbrite.
